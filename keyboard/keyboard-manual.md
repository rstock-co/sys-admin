# YUNZII AL98 Mechanical Keyboard User Manual

### Introduction
The YUNZII AL98 is a high-performance mechanical keyboard designed for gaming and everyday use. Featuring an aluminum alloy body, tri-mode connectivity (Wired, Bluetooth, 2.4G wireless), full-key hot-swappable switches, and customizable RGB lighting, it offers a premium typing experience. This manual provides detailed instructions for setting up, operating, and maintaining your AL98 keyboard.

### Setup

#### Charging the Keyboard

The YUNZII AL98 features a built-in 8000mAh high-capacity lithium battery, offering up to 72 hours of continuous operation. It supports simultaneous charging and use.

1. Connect the provided USB-C cable to the keyboard's USB-C port.
2. Connect the other end of the USB-C cable to a powered USB port on your computer or a USB wall adapter.
3. The keyboard's indicator light will show charging status (refer to LED indicators section for details).

#### Connecting the Keyboard
The AL98 supports three connection modes: Wired, Bluetooth, and 2.4G Wireless.

1. **Wired Connection**

a. Ensure the mode switch on the keyboard (usually on the side or back) is set to 'Wired' or the cable icon.
b. Connect the USB-C cable to the keyboard and the other end to your computer's USB port.
c. The keyboard will be automatically recognized by your system.

2. **2.4G Wireless Connection**

a. Locate the 2.4G wireless receiver (USB dongle) in your package.
b. Plug the 2.4G receiver into an available USB port on your computer.
c. Set the mode switch on the keyboard to '2.4G' or the wireless icon.
d. The keyboard should connect automatically. If not, press and hold the designated pairing key combination (refer to keyboard specific key combinations, usually Fn + 4) until the indicator light flashes, then release.

*Note: If pairing fails, the LOG LED will flash slowly. Reconnect as needed. If no operation occurs for 20 seconds after switching channels, the LOG LED and backlight will turn off. If no operation occurs for 60 seconds in pairing mode, the LOG LED and backlight will turn off. Wake the keyboard before reconnecting.*

3. **Bluetooth Connection**

a. Set the mode switch on the keyboard to 'BT' or the Bluetooth icon.
b. On your device (computer, tablet, phone), enable Bluetooth and search for new devices.
c. Press and hold the designated Bluetooth pairing key combination on the keyboard (e.g., Fn + 1, Fn + 2, or Fn + 3 for different profiles) until the indicator light flashes rapidly.
d. Select 'YUNZII AL98' from the list of available Bluetooth devices on your device.
e. Follow any on-screen prompts to complete the pairing process.

*Note: If pairing fails, the LOG LED will flash slowly. Reconnect as needed. If no operation occurs for 20 seconds after switching channels, the LOG LED and backlight will turn off. If no operation occurs for 60 seconds in pairing mode, the LOG LED and backlight will turn off. Wake the keyboard before reconnecting.*

### Operating Instructions

#### Key Functions and Layout
The AL98 features a 98-key layout, providing a compact design while retaining most of the functionality of a full 101-key keyboard. It supports N-key rollover for precise input.

Many functions are accessed via the 'Fn' key combined with other keys. Specific key combinations may vary; refer to the included quick start guide or the manufacturer's website for a detailed keymap.

#### RGB Backlight Control
The keyboard features RGB full-color backlight with 16.8 million adjustable colors and customizable dynamic lighting effects. Enjoy a vibrant visual experience with rhythmic music and flowing lights.

Use the 'Fn' key in combination with arrow keys or other designated keys (often found on the right side of the keyboard, e.g., Fn + \| for mode, Fn + [ { for brightness) to cycle through lighting modes, adjust brightness, and change colors.

#### QMK/VIA Support for Key Remapping
The YUNZII AL98 supports QMK/VIA firmware, allowing you to customize key remapping, macros, and lighting effects to your preference. This feature provides extensive control over your keyboard's functionality.

To utilize QMK/VIA, do the following:

1. Connect your keyboard to your computer in wired mode, and download the .json file from the YUNZII official website.
2. Open the [VIA website](https://usevia.app/), and click "Authorize Device" to pair the keyboard.
3. Click "SETTINGS" to turn on the "Show Design" tab.
4. Open the DESIGN page on the VIA website, and upload the .json file.
5. After the connection is complete, you can customize your keyboard using the GUI.
6. To remap a key, first click the key you wish to remap on the keyboard, and then click the desired remap button below.  If it worked correctly, you should now see the newly remapped button on the keyboard.

### Troubleshooting

Keyboard not responding: Ensure the keyboard is properly connected (wired, 2.4G receiver, or Bluetooth paired). Check the mode switch. Try restarting your computer.

Wireless connectivity issues: For 2.4G, ensure the receiver is securely plugged in and close to the keyboard. For Bluetooth, ensure your device's Bluetooth is on and the keyboard is paired. Try re-pairing if necessary.

RGB lights not working: Check if the RGB lighting is turned off or set to minimum brightness using the Fn key combinations. Ensure the keyboard has sufficient battery charge or is connected via USB.

Keys not registering: If a specific key is not working, try hot-swapping the switch with a known working one to determine if the issue is with the switch or the PCB.

Battery not charging: Ensure the USB-C cable is fully inserted and connected to a working power source. Try a different cable or USB port.

### Battery Information

#### Low Power Mode & Charging
When the battery is below 10%, the LOG LED will flash red quickly. When charging, the LOG LED will stay on red, and then restore to the normal lighting effect; When the keyboard is fully charged, the LOG LED will stay on solid green for 5 seconds before returning to normal lighting mode.

*Note: Charging time may vary slightly based on power output.*

#### Battery Check
In 2.4G/Bluetooth mode, press `Fn+Right Ctrl` to check the battery level. The backlight and LOG LED will turn off, and the number keys 1-0 will illuminate to show the power percentage. Releasing the key returns the keyboard to its previous backlight mode.

#### Power Saving Mode
In wireless modes (Bluetooth/2.4G), the keyboard enters sleep mode after 5 minutes of inactivity, turning off the lights while maintaining the connection. Pressing a key wakes the keyboard and outputs characters simultaneously. After 30 minutes of inactivity, the keyboard enters deep sleep mode, disconnecting and requiring a key press to wake. The keyboard will automatically reconnect upon waking.
